I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Chicago advised be Prof. Shan Lu. I am also fortunate to collaborate with and be mentored by Dr. Suman Nath and Dr. Madan Musuvathi from Microsoft Research. Broadly, my research focuses on improving the correctness and performance of large-scale distributed systems. In particular, I combine program analysis, code instrumentation, fault injection and execution profiling to help developers better reason about their code and isolate bugs faster.

Before joining the University of Chicago, I obtained my MSc degree from the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Lausanne (EPFL) and my BSc degree from the University of Bucharest.
